Puzzle Game

    Welcome to match 2 puzzle game! Your goal is to match each of the pictures with its copy, 
    Click the two matching images to reveal them. But be careful, there's a time limit!
    To start, select how many pictures you want to play, then select the difficulty level.
    Finally click Begin to play!
    You can stop anytime with the Stop button below the timer.
    Good luck, have fun!
